# Nmap Initial SMTP Scan

Command:
nmap -p25 -sV -sC <TARGET-IP>

Purpose:
- Detect SMTP service
- Identify banner
- Identify SMTP capabilities
- Extract domain information

Findings:
- SMTP service running on port 25
- ESMTP detected
- Domain identified:
  inlanefreight.htb

Notes:
The SMTP banner provided useful footprinting information that later helped during username enumeration.
